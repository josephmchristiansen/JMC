RECREATIONAL APPLICATIONS

AI COACHING

Athletic teams and individuals can use Artificial Intelligence, to evaluate minute details in large samples of data and AI can do this in real time. While people can use statistics to look through large amounts of data to get patterns, AI can search through massive amounts of data to pick up on minuscule details to identify essential trends; any human may easily miss these tiny details.

FITNESS TRACKER

Artificial Intelligence can be used to track your fitness and health. This may not be news to some; however, this technology is not only implemented in smartwatches and other wrist-worn items. There are AI systems that have been built into earbuds; this AI system can determine speed, heartbeat, altitude, and many other factors. All of which you can be told through the earbuds.

AI CAMERAMAN

Artificial Intelligence can be used by athletes to watch their technique and get action shots without having to worry about bulky drone remotes. The AI system aboard a drone can be used to track the user and capture high-resolution video and photos. Along with an autonomous following system, the user can also turn on a manual flight mode so the user can fly the drone with their smartphone to capture other types of scenes.
