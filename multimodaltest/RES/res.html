RESEARCH APPLICATIONS

HEALTHCARE RESEARCH

Healthcare can benefit from Artificial Intelligence; AI is helping healthcare by linking and creating neural networks. AI also can ensure that all this information is sorted, organized, and secure. Artificial Intelligence is of massive importance to the healthcare system, as it can comb through immense amounts of data with little effort put forward by any humans; allowing them to focus on helping people.

AI CURING CANCER

Artificial Intelligence is being used to search for a cure, or an effective treatment, for cancer, one of the healthcare industry’s most prominent difficulties. While human researchers can look at old case studies and make inferences based on those studies, AI researchers can look through millions of case studies and create more in-depth conclusions based on those studies in a fraction of the time the human researchers would take.

AI MARKETING RESEARCH

Artificial Intelligence can be used to forecast everything that a business might need or want. AI can look through previous years’ sales reports to identify what products will be more heavily demanded and what products will have lower demand. An AI system can also analyze the levels of work from each employee from previous years to determine the ideal number of employees to have scheduled for each day.
