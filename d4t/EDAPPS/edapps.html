EDUCATIONAL APPLICATIONS

LEARNING

Artificial intelligence (AI) can be used in the classroom to personalize the learning process in a way that is difficult for a teacher who has to address the needs of various students. This individualized learning would make the learning process more efficient, accelerating student growth.

GROUPS

AI can be used in the classroom to provide better group formation. By analyzing the individual strengths of each student, AI would be able to produce groups that would excel in various topics. AI would also be able to build well-rounded groups by placing students who are weak in certain topics with other students who consider that topic a strength.

SCHEDULING

AI can be used in the classroom to provide dynamic scheduling; by studying individual student habits, the AI will be able to predict and calculate the best studying schedule for that individual. As every person is different this will help ensure every student has a custom schedule that helps them achieve their goals and get their work completed in a timely manner.
